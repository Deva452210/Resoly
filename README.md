# Overview

Resoly is an AI-powered civic governance platform that transforms the way citizens, government officers, and authorities collaborate to solve public issues.

Instead of simply collecting complaints, Resoly embeds Artificial Intelligence throughout the entire civic lifecycle—from complaint generation to executive decision support.

---

# The Problem

Traditional civic complaint systems suffer from:

- Citizens don't know the correct department
- Poor complaint descriptions
- Manual verification
- Slow resolution
- No intelligent prioritization
- Limited executive insights

# ✨ AI Features

## AI Complaint Generation

- Image understanding
- Auto-generated title
- Auto-generated description
- Department prediction
- Category prediction
- Priority estimation

---

## AI Investigation Agent

AI interviews the citizen using intelligent follow-up questions before creating the complaint.

---

## AI Resolution Auditor

Compares before and after images to determine repair quality.

Provides

- Confidence score
- Summary
- Remaining issues
- Recommendations

---

## AI Executive Brief

Provides city-wide summaries including

- Complaint statistics
- Officer insights
- Trends
- Recommendations

---

## AI Civic Intelligence

Analyzes complaint relationships to identify

- Emerging hotspots
- Related complaints
- Root causes
- Recommended actions

---

## AI Governance Copilot

A conversational AI assistant for higher authorities that answers natural language questions using Resoly's civic data.
